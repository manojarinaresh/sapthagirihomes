Best Areas to Live in Tirupati
— Mana-Properties

Your complete guide to Tirupati's top residential neighbourhoods — from IIT corridors to spiritual foothills.

Local Guide July 2026 8 min read Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh
Tirupati — the sacred city nestled at the foot of the Tirumala hills — is no longer just a pilgrimage destination. With the rise of YSRTP Industrial Corridor, IIT Tirupati, and improved NH connectivity, it has emerged as Andhra Pradesh's fastest-growing residential city. Whether you're a first-time buyer, an investor, or a family seeking a peaceful yet connected home, Tirupati offers neighbourhoods for every budget and lifestyle.

At Mana-Properties, we connect buyers directly with owners — zero brokerage, 100% transparency. Here are the top 10 areas to live in Tirupati right now.

Yerpedu – IIT Corridor

Premium Investment
IIT Tirupati Campus NH-16 Frontage High ROI Gated Communities

Yerpedu has transformed into Tirupati's most sought-after investment corridor since IIT Tirupati established its permanent campus here. Flanking the NH-16 highway, this zone offers premium gated communities, plotted developments, and apartment projects with world-class infrastructure. Proximity to the upcoming YSRTP Industrial Corridor makes it a dual-demand zone — faculty housing and industrial workforce rentals both drive strong yields.

Connectivity is outstanding: 15 minutes to Tirupati Central, 20 minutes to Renigunta Airport, and direct highway access to Chennai and Hyderabad. Ideal for long-term investors and professionals.

Renigunta / Airport Zone

Connectivity Hub
Tirupati Airport Railway Junction Commercial Growth Business Travellers

Renigunta is the gateway to Tirupati — home to both the international airport and a major railway junction. This strategic location makes it the preferred address for business travellers, logistics professionals, and families who value connectivity. Rental demand is consistently high, driven by airport staff, airline crew, and frequent pilgrim footfall.

Affordable apartments and independent houses dominate the market here. The upcoming airport expansion and new commercial complexes are set to push property values significantly over the next 3–5 years.

Chandragiri

Heritage & Affordable
Chandragiri Fort Affordable Plots First-Time Buyers Serene Lifestyle

Chandragiri carries the weight of Vijayanagara history — the famous Chandragiri Fort and Raja Mahal draw history buffs and tourists year-round. For home buyers, it's the city's best-kept secret: large plots at affordable prices, independent villas, and a genuinely peaceful living environment just 15 km from Tirupati city centre.

With improving road infrastructure and a growing local economy, Chandragiri is ideal for buyers seeking value-for-money purchases with long-term appreciation potential. Perfect for joint families and retirees.

Tiruchanoor

Spiritual & Family
Padmavathi Temple Family Villas Peaceful Living Good Schools

Home to the celebrated Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple, Tiruchanoor is deeply spiritual yet exceptionally well-connected. Families who value daily spiritual practice, quiet neighbourhoods, and good schools choose Tiruchanoor overwhelmingly. The area has seen rapid villa and apartment development over the last decade.

Located just 5 km from Tirupati city on the Tirupati–Tiruchanoor Road, it combines the serenity of a temple town with easy urban access. Increasingly popular with NRI buyers looking to retire near the divine foothills.

Narayanadri Hospital Road

Medical Hub
Narayanadri Hospital Medical Professionals Healthcare Corridor High Rental Demand

The stretch along Narayanadri Hospital Road has evolved into a thriving medical ecosystem. Doctors, nurses, hospital administrators, and medical students form a large rental base, creating consistently strong occupancy rates for investors. Multiple clinics, pharmacies, and diagnostic centres line the road.

Apartments and furnished rooms along this corridor command premium rents. For owner-occupants in the healthcare sector, living minutes from one of Tirupati's leading hospitals is a practical priority. Excellent for rental investors targeting medical professionals.

Alipiri Foothills

Scenic Luxury
Tirumala Foothills Luxury Villas Panoramic Views NRI Favourite

Alipiri is where Tirupati's flat lands meet the sacred Tirumala hills. Waking up to panoramic views of the seven hills, cool breezes, and the ambient chanting from the footpath pilgrims is a lifestyle that money can barely replicate. Luxury villa communities and premium independent houses dominate here.

This is Tirupati's most premium residential zone — comparable to Mumbai's Malabar Hill or Hyderabad's Jubilee Hills in terms of prestige. NRI buyers, retired IAS/IPS officers, and temple trust employees prefer Alipiri. Limited supply ensures values remain strong.

SV University Area

Education Hub
Sri Venkateswara University Student Housing High Rental Demand PG / Apartments

Sri Venkateswara University (SVU) — one of Andhra Pradesh's most prestigious institutions — anchors this zone. Student hostels, PG accommodations, and compact apartments cater to thousands of students and faculty members. Investors in this zone enjoy near-zero vacancy rates throughout the academic year.

The area has also attracted coaching institutes, bookshops, cafes, and convenience retail, creating a vibrant micro-economy. 1 BHK and 2 BHK apartments here offer some of the highest rental yields in the entire Tirupati district.

Padmavatipuram

Central Living
City Centre Walk to Markets TSRTC Connectivity All Amenities Close

Padmavatipuram sits right at the heart of Tirupati city, offering the quintessential urban convenience: within walking distance of major hospitals, markets, government offices, banks, and schools. Apartments here are compact but exceptionally well-located, ideal for salaried professionals and nuclear families who prioritise time over space.

The area's central position means most daily errands require no vehicle. For buyers unable to afford Alipiri but wanting a genuinely central address, Padmavatipuram is the natural choice.

KT Road

Prime Residential
Kalyani Talkies Road Established Area Mid-Premium Good Infrastructure

KT Road (Kalyani Talkies Road) is one of Tirupati's most established residential arteries. With wide roads, mature trees, and decades of residential development, it offers a pleasant, settled neighbourhood feel. Mid-segment apartments and independent houses here are perennially in demand among government employees, teachers, and established business families.

Consistent rental income, good school access, and proximity to commercial areas make KT Road a safe, low-risk investment. Values have appreciated steadily without the volatility of newer zones.

Karakambadi Road

Industrial Corridor
Industrial Zone Affordable Plots Future Growth Large Land Parcels

Karakambadi Road runs towards the industrial outskirts of Tirupati, making it the destination of choice for industrial workers, logistics staff, and buyers seeking large plot sizes at budget prices. Several SEZ-adjacent plots and APSIDC-allocated industrial parcels have driven residential supply in this corridor.

This is Tirupati's emerging frontier — current prices are low, but with industrial expansion and road-widening projects underway, the capital appreciation potential over a 5–10 year horizon is significant. Best for early-stage investors and those seeking affordable independent homes.
